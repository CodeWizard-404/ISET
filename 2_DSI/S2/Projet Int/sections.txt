1. **Mathématiques et Physique (MP)**
   - **Description**: This stream focuses on advanced mathematics and physics.
   - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
   - **Subjects**: Mathematics, Physics

2. **Physique et Technologie (PT)**
   - **Description**: Emphasizes physics and technology.
   - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
   - **Subjects**: Physics, Technology

3. **Sciences de l'Ingénieur (SI)**
   - **Description**: Covers a combination of mathematics, physics, and engineering-related subjects.
   - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
   - **Subjects**: Mathematics, Physics, Engineering Sciences

4. **Biologie, Chimie et Géologie (BCG)**
   - **Description**: Focuses on biology, chemistry, and geology.
   - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
   - **Subjects**: Biology, Chemistry, Geology

5. **Économie et Gestion (EG)**
   - **Description**: Integrates economics and management with mathematics.
   - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
   - **Subjects**: Mathematics, Economics, Management

6. **Lettres et Sciences Sociales (LSS)**
   - **Description**: Covers literature and social sciences.
   - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
   - **Subjects**: Literature, Social Sciences

7. **Lettres et Sciences Humaines (LSH)**
   - **Description**: Focuses on literature and humanities.
   - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
   - **Subjects**: Literature, Humanities

8. **Mathématiques, Informatique et Sciences de l'Ingénieur (MPSI)**
   - **Description**: Includes mathematics, computer science, and engineering sciences.
   - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
   - **Subjects**: Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering Sciences

9. **Mathématiques, Physique et Sciences de l'Ingénieur (MPSI)**
   - **Description**: Emphasizes mathematics, physics, and engineering sciences.
   - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
   - **Subjects**: Mathematics, Physics, Engineering Sciences

10. **Physique, Chimie et Sciences de l'Ingénieur (PCSI)**
    - **Description**: Covers physics, chemistry, and engineering sciences.
    - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
    - **Subjects**: Physics, Chemistry, Engineering Sciences

11. **Physique, Chimie et Technologie (PCT)**
    - **Description**: Focuses on physics, chemistry, and technology.
    - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
    - **Subjects**: Physics, Chemistry, Technology

12. **Biologie, Chimie, Physique et Sciences de la Terre (BCPST)**
    - **Description**: Integrates biology, chemistry, physics, and earth sciences.
    - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
    - **Subjects**: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Earth Sciences

13. **Technologie et Sciences Industrielles (TSI)**
    - **Description**: Emphasizes technology and industrial sciences.
    - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
    - **Subjects**: Technology, Industrial Sciences

14. **Technologie, Sciences Industrielles et Développement Durable (TSIDD)**
    - **Description**: Integrates technology, industrial sciences, and sustainable development.
    - **Level**: First Year (1ère Année)
    - **Subjects**: Technology, Industrial Sciences, Sustainable Development
